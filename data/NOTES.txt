Data Notes
==========

Bird 2
------
Syllables 'g' and 'a' were originally segmented as a single chunk labelled 'a'.
In bird_2_df.csv, every 'a' has been expanded to 'ga' in the song column to
reflect the true syllable composition. However, the onsets and offsets columns
still reflect the original single-chunk segmentation — there is one onset/offset
pair for the 'ga' chunk, not separate values for 'g' and 'a'.
